CWO

Computational Weapon Optic. The Digital Smart-Scope.

The Computational Weapon Optic (CWO) is a rifle scope with smart-phone capability. Apps bring a ballistic solution, synchronized fire, fratricide detection, and command and control capabilities to the mobile infantry.
